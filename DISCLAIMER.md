# Medical Disclaimer

CESA is a software engineering demonstration. It retrieves and summarizes
peer-reviewed literature indexed in PubMed (via NCBI E-utilities) for informational
and educational purposes only.

- CESA does **not** provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
- Nothing produced by CESA is a substitute for professional medical judgment.
  Always consult a qualified healthcare provider.
- CESA may retrieve incomplete, outdated, or contextually inappropriate literature,
  and LLM synthesis may contain errors despite citation checks.
- CESA is not a medical device and has not been evaluated by any regulatory body.
